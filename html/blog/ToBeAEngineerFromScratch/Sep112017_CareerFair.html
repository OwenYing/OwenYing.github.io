Career Fair First Time Sep 11, 2017

Career Fair Sep 11, 2017

This is the first time that I attend a career fair. A little bit excited and nervous too. I am looking for 2018 summer's internship. I made a list of companies that I must apply to. These are Facebook, Amazon, Bloomberg, Microsoft, Visa, IBM .... Lots of small companies cannot sponsor international students. So international students' choice is not wide.

Most of the companies collect my resume and still need to apply online. Firstly, I am a little nervous so I choose some small companies to start with as Walmart Tech and etc. After talking to some recruiters, I am feeling prepared.

The first company I go to is Facebook. They collect your e-resume and it's done. Most of the companies do this, and ask about your resume.

The most different company is Bloomberg. They quickly review your resume and then ask you algorithms. My question is find missing elements in sorted array. eg: [1,2,5,8], then you need to return [3,4,6,7]
I done it by

  • Make an example
  • Write down my steps of the algo as comments
  • Name the variable nicely
  • Solve the problem

After the day, I applied 10+ companies and gets lots of souvenirs, Yeah!!
And I got an interview from Bloomberg.
